About me

I live in Oslo and work in admin at a theatre. This may sound like an exciting place to work to some, but it is mainly like any other office job – except you sometimes see people dressed in weird costumes at lunch time.

Interests and hobbies

I am at my happiest when I can make stuff by hand; sewing and knitting, woodwork, cooking, and gardening. I am always on the lookout for new projects, but at the same time trying to get better at completing the ones I have started. Learning new skills is the best feeling there is!

3 fun facts about me

  • I hold the unofficial world record for most bananas peeled in one minute.
  • I was actually raised by a family of badgers until the age of five, when my foster family found me in the forest while foraging for mushrooms.
  • I have a compulsive urge to lie when asked to say something fun about myself.
